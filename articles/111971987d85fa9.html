      Reading traffic news

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

The incident involves two men, and members of the public have been asked to avoid the area

Due to an ongoing incident today, police in riot gear have been spotted on a Reading street, and residents are being advised to avoid the area (Thursday, December 8). A major road is closed, and emergency crews are still on the scene, where two men are said to be involved.

Caversham Road on the A4155 is closed in both directions, and traffic is backed up. The road is closed between the A329 Vastern Road and Richfield Avenue.

The road was first reported closed around 6.30 a.m. Thames Valley Police officers and Royal Berkshire Fire and Rescue Service firefighters are on the scene.




The aftermath of a Reading police incident that caused Caversham Road to be closed for over four hours

"Thames Valley Police are currently dealing with an incident on Caversham Road involving two men," a Thames Valley Police spokesperson said. The road between Richfield Avenue and the A329 Vastern Road is currently closed. Please avoid the area and take a different route."

"We are currently responding to an incident in Caversham Road, Reading," said a spokesperson for Royal Berkshire Fire and Rescue Service. Please stay away from the area.
