Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Following a 'police incident' today, a major road in Reading's town center has reopened (Thursday, December 8). Caversham Road was closed between Vastern Road and Caversham Bridge Roundabout after police, including riot officers, arrived.

The road was first reported closed around 6.30 a.m. By 10.40 a.m., police said it was reopened to traffic.

Following the incident, three men were arrested. Thames Valley Police stated that no one else is being sought in connection with the incident.

"Following the incident in Caversham Road this morning, three men have now been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit burglary," a Thames Valley Police spokesperson said. Caversham Road is now open, and officers will remain on the scene to reassure residents.
