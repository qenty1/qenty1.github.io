      Caversham bridge closed

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Caversham Road has now reopened following the earlier incident. "Following the incident in Caversham Road this morning, three men have now been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit burglary," Thames Valley Police said on social media. Caversham Road is now open, and officers will remain in the area to reassure residents.

COMMUTERS are having a difficult morning after police closed a road in Reading.

Caversham Bridge Road is closed between the TGI Friday roundabout and the Caversham Bridge roundabout. It was closed around 6.30 a.m. on Thursday, December 8.

Royal Berkshire Fire and Rescue Service is on the scene and is advising residents to avoid the area.




A road traffic collision has also been reported on Caversham Bridge, adding to the delays. As a result, traffic has been backed up throughout the Reading area all morning.

There are delays along the Oxford Road up to Tilehurst railway station, along the IDR right up to the A33, across Caversham as people try to cross the roads, and along Richfield Avenue.

Thames Valley Police has not yet responded to the closure.
