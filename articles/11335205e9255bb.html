      West Penn Township

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Around 3:45pm, Wednesday, emergency personnel were called to 1121 Clamtown Road/Route 443, West Penn Township, near Tamaqua for a structure fire with possible entrapment.

The single-home fire drew firefighters from Schuylkill and neighboring counties, as well as a tanker task force.

About an hour into the incident, firefighters on the second floor issued two maydays. The RIT rushed into the fire to save the two firefighters, who were then transported to St. Luke's Miners Hospital in Coaldale.




Unfortunately, both firefighters died as a result of their injuries and were pronounced dead at the hospital by the Schuylkill County Coroner's Office. According to the Coroner's Office, the firefighters were from New Tripoli.

The Coroner's office was also called to the fire scene after the body of a third person was discovered near the scene of an apparent self-inflicted gunshot wound.
