      Schuylkill county fire

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

A house fire in Schuylkill County killed three people, including two firefighters, on Wednesday afternoon.

At around 3:45 p.m., a fatal fire broke out on the 1100 block of Clamtown Road in West Penn Township, near the town of Tamaqua.

The firefighters became trapped while fighting the fire inside the house. A mayday signal was issued. Both firefighters were rescued but died as a result of their injuries.

The coroner pronounced a third person, who was not a firefighter, dead on the scene.




Both firefighters were Lehigh County residents.

At this time, the cause of the fire is unknown. West Penn Township Police and the Pennsylvania State Police fire marshal are looking into the incident.
