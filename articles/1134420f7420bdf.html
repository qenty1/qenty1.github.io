      New tripoli fire company

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Two firefighters died Wednesday while battling a house fire in West Brunswick Township, Schuylkill County, and a third person was found dead from a gunshot wound.

Two New Tripoli firefighters were dragged from the structure and taken to ambulances. They were pronounced dead at St. Luke's Hospital-Miners Campus in Coaldale at 6:10 p.m.

According to authorities, a resident of the house was discovered dead in the woods behind the structure, apparently from a self-inflicted gunshot wound.




Officials have confirmed the deaths of two firefighters.

Firefighters from across Schuylkill County are still battling a fire in West Penn Township near Tamaqua.
