      Caversham accident today

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Following an incident on Thursday morning, a busy Reading road has reopened. Police, including riot officers, were called to Caversham Road.

The road was closed to traffic for more than four hours while officers investigated. The incident began around 6.30 a.m.

Three men have been arrested and are being held. Thames Valley Police stated that officers are still on the scene.

According to a Thames Valley Police spokesperson: "Following this morning's incident on Caversham Road, three men have been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit burglary. Caversham Road is now open, and officers will remain on the scene to reassure residents.




Aftermath of Reading police incident that saw Caversham Road shut for more than four hours

"We are not seeking any further people in relation to this incident. We are working with our partners to return the area to normality and thank the public for their patience whilst we dealt with this incident.
