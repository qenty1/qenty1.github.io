      Travis pastrana accident

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Travis Pastrana Injury: Travis Pastrana is currently recovering from injuries sustained during a dangerous parachute stunt. The motorsports competitor and stuntman, 38, was taken to a hospital in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, on Saturday after attempting to jump from a high-rise downtown hotel to Huizenga Plaza below.

Pastrana can be seen in an Instagram video of the incident attempting to maintain control of his parachute as he falls toward the ground after jumping from the top of the hotel and rapidly gathering speed as he descends toward the ground. The film concludes with a shot of the 11-time X Games gold medalist landing hard on the grass near the hotel. This shot can be seen in the video's final few seconds.

According to Pastrana's publicist, who spoke to WSVN, the stuntman, who was filming a web series at the time of the accident, fractured his pelvis in the accident and required surgery. The extreme sports enthusiast, who was named as one of three judges on America's Got Talent: Extreme in October before production was halted due to a stunt going wrong, has a history of attempting fear-inducing stunts, including jumping out of a plane without a parachute in 2007.




"That was more of a pass-or-fail situation." It was more of a trust test to see if my parachuting buddies liked me enough to catch me as I fell from the sky." Pastrana revealed the information about the stunt to The Guardian in 2019.

"It turned out to be illegal, and I got into a lot of trouble," he continued, adding, "Oddly enough, jumping out of a plane without a parachute is not the dangerous thing; the most terrifying part is clutching onto your friend who is wearing a parachute and hoping you don't fall."
