      New york city tourism

Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

With the addition of new cruises to Brooklyn Terminal, New York City will see a significant increase in tourism.

Mayor Eric Adams says that beginning in April, travelers will be able to take MSC Cruises from Red Hook to Bermuda, the Bahamas, and Florida, as well as up to Canada and New England.

"We anticipate 219,000 MSC Cruises passengers arriving in New York City each year, generating... an estimated $102 million in spending in our restaurants, shops, and bars," Adams said.




It is expected that the cruises will generate 10,000 new jobs in the city.

In addition, MSC Cruises is donating $236,000 to Red Hook Green Thumb Gardens and the city's junior ambassador program.
